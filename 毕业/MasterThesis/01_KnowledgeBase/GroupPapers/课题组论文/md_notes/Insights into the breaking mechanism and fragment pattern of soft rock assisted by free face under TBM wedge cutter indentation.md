1. Introduction
In the 21st century, tunnel and underground space engineering has been developing rapidly, such as railway, urban rail, hydro-
power tunnels, water conveyance tunnels, underground pipe galleries and other tunnels. For large-scale water conservancy, trans-
portation, oil and gas projects, such as inter-basin water transfer and railway projects crossing mountains, the traditional drilling-
blasting method has been difficult to apply to their constructions due to its low excavation efficiency, poor construction environ-
ment and personnel safety, etc. Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) has become the mainstream technology and development direction of
tunnel construction with its advantages of safety, rapidity, high efficiency, environmental protection, and high comprehensive benefits
[1,2]. However, traditional TBM cutterhead has a low rock breaking efficiency and high project cost due to severe tool wear in the
excavation of deep tunnels [3].
